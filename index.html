Silicon Valley Bank's Troubles Spark Concerns for Tech Startup Market

Silicon Valley Bank is a major funder for tech startups and has suffered significant losses due to its close association with the tech industry. The bank was forced to sell $21 billion in securities at a $1.8 billion loss and received $500 million from General Atlantic. After a 75% surge in 2021, the bank lost two-thirds of its value in 2022 and plummeted 60% on Thursday.
